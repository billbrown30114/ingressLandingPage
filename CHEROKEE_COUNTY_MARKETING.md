# Cherokee County Marketing Strategy
## Canton & Woodstock, GA - Industry-Specific Messaging

### Market Overview
- **Population**: 293,513 (Cherokee County), 38,049 (Canton), 39,381 (Woodstock)
- **Median Family Income**: $105,442
- **Education**: 92% high school graduates, 40% bachelor's degree or higher
- **Unemployment**: 2.7%
- **Growth**: 30.45% population increase since 2015
